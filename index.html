    Event Details
    Where the TECH is without limit


    All India IEEE Computer Society Students and Young professionals' Congress, AICSSYC '22, is the largest IEEE event held annually in India, to provide a platform for students, young professionals and leaders who are trailblazers in their respective fields, to come together and contribute to the melting pot of technology and scientific fervour. To be a part of this yearly spectacle is thus, an honour in itself and a rare chance to realise where one's true potential lies. The takeaways of knowledge and experience from this massive event will go a long way in fueling one's journey towards being a distinguished member of the field one is passionate about pursuing.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    How many days will the event be organised for?

    AICSSYC '22 is going to be a 3-day event. They finalized dates are the 17th,18th and 19th of December. Chilly days, sure but we hope our hospitality will warm you.

    What is the registration fees to participate in the event?

    Nominal. We are interested in your mind and it's enthusiasm. Not your coffers.(Further details will be revealed soon)

    How many members are needed in each team?

    You and the grace of God.(Further details will be released soon)

    What are the eligibility criteria for participating?

    Any young tech enthusiast with a passion of tech and a vigour for innovation can participate. We welcome everybody, especially the new-comers who are getting started on their professional or academic careers.

    What is the venue for the event?

    The event will be based in Bengal in a scenic and hospitable accommodation facility which can house over 400 tech enthusiasts for a 3-day galore. (Further details will be revealed soon.)

    When is the deadline to register?

    "Kal karein so aaj kar,aaj karein so ab"-Saint Kabir

    What are the prizes for the winners?

    Hefty. Very hefty. This is a national scale flagship event hosted by the oldest and most prestigious branch of IEEE- the CS Society.(Furthur details will be released soon)

    What will I gain out of participation in the event?

    Exposure, market and industry connections, intellectual stimulation,lifelong experience,you name it. To be honest,the real question is-what can't you gain out of participating in a national scale event of the level of prestige that AICSSYC holds in the tech industry fraternity of India with the brightest minds in the country? The answer - You are only bounded by your own limits in this event, so come on over and help us realize our motto "Where the Tech is without Limits"
