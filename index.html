    Event Details
    The Biggest Eastern India Networking Event


    When it comes to being a party to a conglomeration of various ideas freely flowing in from like-minded individuals spread across the world, a conference can never go out of style. And when it comes to global leaders associating for several common causes that may impact the society, the opportunity of learning and networking that springs up for the attendees is of paramount importance. AICSSYC, the very first of its kind, organized by IEEE JU SB brings you the golden opportunity to learn, connect and advance further towards your aspirations with the roadmap to be provided by our global leaders who will grace the event.
